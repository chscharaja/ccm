About Us

Welcome to the Chhatrapati Cha Maharaja! Formerly known as Siddhivinayak Friends Association, we are a devoted community celebrating the divine presence of Lord Ganesha. With a rich history and tradition, we come together to honor the elephant-headed deity in all his glory. Explore our site to learn more about our vibrant festivities, cultural heritage, and the spiritual significance of Ganesha in our lives. Join us in our joyous celebrations and immerse yourself in the captivating world of Chhatrapati Cha Maharaja Ganesh Mandal.
